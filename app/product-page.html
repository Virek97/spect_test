Spectrolab Panel pressure regulator PM 51

Product Features

  • Brass (chrome plated) panel pressure regulator for panel and panel surface mounting

  • For non-corrosive gases and gas mixtures up to quality 6.0

  • 2, 4 or 6 ports for flexible and individual configuration

  • Very stable outlet pressure

  • Suitable for inlet pressures up to 300 bar

  • Max. outlet pressures 1,5 up to 200 bar

  • Metal-to-metal seal to atmosphere

  • Suitable for ECD-applications

  • Pressure regulator can be evacuated

  • Inlet filter

  • Approved for use with oxygen

  • Simple outlet pressure limitation by handwheel

  • Easy to install

  • New laboratory-style design

  • Ergonomically designed

Gas supply equipment for non corrosive gases and gas mixtures up to grade 6.0
